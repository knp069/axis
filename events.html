  1. Q. What is the difference in animation 2D and 3D?

    The most obvious difference between the two genres of animation is the three dimensional characteristics or the appearance of depth. 2D animation is created by drawing almost every frame of the animated movie and is the base of 3D animation. 3D animation is created by changing the poses and the placement of already created 3D models. It has more depth and is more realistic, it is a series of images/ objects created using computers or any other digital media.

Remember Shah Rukh Khan’s movie SWADES which received an overwhelmingly strong critical acclaim around the globe where he sets up a small hydro-electric power plant that would solve the problem of irregular electricity and make the village self-sufficient. That’s what define the event TURBO FLUX.

The basic applications of fluid mechanics is to be applied where the water is to be progressed from one point to another passing through various obstacles so that it falls over the turbine with sufficient velocity, rotates it and thus producing significant electricity. Knowledge of high school fluid mechanics and mere capability of application of them are just its prerequisite.

Think…Which pipe will you prefer V-shaped or a U-shaped for flow of water??

PROBLEM STATEMENT

Participants have to take the water to a certain height using suction pumps (arena is shown in the fig.) overcoming obstacles in the path and various hurdles, as in water reaches the highest level with minimum losses. To measure the energy possessed with the water, it is gushed on to the turbine which is coupled to dynamo and the voltage generated is measured.

The obsctales will be disclosed on the day of the event and the sequence of the obsctacles will be decided by the participants.

RULES

  1. Maximum Number of participants per team : 3
  2. Participants should come with a turbine.
    3. Turbine specifications:
    1. 1.Diameter of turbine should not be more than 80 cm
    2. 2.Bore diameter should be 6 mm.
    3. 3.There is no restriction regarding type of material you use for making turbine.
    4. 4.Weight of turbine should not be more than 1.5 kg.
  4. The turbine should be strictly to the specification we have provided. Deviation from it may cause disqualification.
  5. The arrangement of the obsctacles and the pumps on the arena as well as mounting of the turbine will be done by the Turbo Flux organisers.
  6. The participants will be allowed to control the time at which they want the pumps to be switched on.
  7. Once the sequence of obstacles decided, no changes will be allowed.
  8. Points will be there for using lesser number of pumps than allowed.

JUDGING CRATERIA

  1. Time water takes from start to reach to turbine.
  2. Voltage generated due to rotation of turbine.
  3. Meanwhile, other minor criteria will be revealed that day itself.
