Campus Ambassador

Campus Ambassador is a person who shall be solely responsible for publicizing our technical festival AXIS'15, VNIT Nagpur in his/ her college. The ambassador shall not only be an integral part of our publicity team but also shall be the face of the festival in his/her college. It is a great opportunity for the ambassadors to enhance their skills of publicity, managing skills, leadership and maintaining public relations.

The following are the duties of a Campus Ambassador

  • 1. Put up our posters on all the notice boards in the college.
  • 2. Spread our messages among all the contacts on whatsapp
  • 3. Share and publicize our facebook posts.

Benefits of being a Campus Ambassadors

  • 1. Each Ambassador shall get a certificate for putting their efforts to publicize our festival
  • 2. An opportunity to interact with students from all over the nation.
  • 3. To become a part of a huge network of students and get an experience in management skills and public relations.
